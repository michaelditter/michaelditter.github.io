About Me

Experienced leader at the intersection of AI, technology, and consumer experiences

Michael J Ditter

Director of AI Strategy and Emerging Technology at Diageo

I am a strategic leader specializing in AI implementation, emerging technology integration, and innovative consumer experiences. Currently serving as the Director of AI Strategy and Emerging Technology at Diageo, I lead initiatives to drive technological innovation and transformative AI partnerships across iconic global brands.

With a background spanning entrepreneurship and corporate leadership, I've successfully built and scaled businesses, including Partay (a Ready-to-Drink beverage brand) and BevSpark.ai (an AI-driven platform for the beverage industry). I've also pioneered immersive experiences for cutting-edge platforms like Apple Vision Pro.

My passion lies in pushing boundaries and creating innovative solutions at the intersection of AI, AR/VR, and consumer engagement. I specialize in developing strategies that help brands connect with consumers in bold, new ways while driving business growth through technological advancement.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with me

How do you approach new AI strategy engagements?

I begin by thoroughly understanding your business objectives, current technological landscape, and organizational readiness. This initial assessment allows me to identify high-impact opportunities for AI integration that align with your strategic goals. From there, I develop a phased implementation roadmap that prioritizes initiatives based on potential value, feasibility, and time-to-impact.

What industries do you specialize in?

I have extensive experience in consumer goods, beverage, retail, and technology sectors. However, my methodologies and approaches to AI and emerging technology strategy are applicable across industries. The fundamental principles of effective technology integration, change management, and digital transformation remain consistent, though adaptations are made to address industry-specific challenges and opportunities.

How long do typical projects take?

Project timelines vary significantly based on scope and complexity. Initial strategy development typically takes 4-8 weeks, while implementation phases can range from 3-12 months depending on the scale of the initiative. I structure engagements to deliver incremental value throughout the process, rather than waiting until the end to demonstrate results.

How do you measure success in AI implementation?

Success metrics are tailored to your specific objectives but typically include a blend of quantitative and qualitative indicators. These might include efficiency gains, cost reduction, revenue growth, improved decision quality, enhanced customer experience, and increased employee satisfaction. I work with clients to establish baseline measurements and implement tracking mechanisms to demonstrate clear ROI.

What makes your approach to AR/VR unique?

My approach to AR/VR focuses on business value first, technology second. Many organizations are enamored with the technology itself rather than understanding how it can solve real business problems. I help clients identify concrete use cases where immersive technologies can deliver measurable value, whether through enhanced customer engagement, improved training outcomes, or operational efficiencies. This pragmatic approach ensures sustainable adoption and tangible results.

Do you offer ongoing support after strategy development?

Yes, I offer various support models based on client needs. These range from periodic check-ins and advisement to more involved implementation oversight and capability building. Many clients benefit from an ongoing advisory relationship where I provide guidance on emerging trends, technology evolution, and opportunity identification. This ensures that strategies remain relevant and effective as the technological landscape evolves.
